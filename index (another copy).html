About Me

Hello there! I studied Electronics and Microsystems Engineering at OTH Regensburg, where I discovered my deep enthusiasm for robotics, automation, and embedded systems. I am passionate about exploring intelligent technologies that bridge hardware and software to bring machines to life. My curiosity and dedication have driven me to continuously learn, build, and contribute to impactful projects in the field of autonomous systems.

My professional journey started with a strong foundation in electronics and communication, combined with a growing interest in mechatronics. Over time, I developed hands-on experience in programming with C, C++, Python, and working in embedded systems and Linux environments. My work spans robotic system integration, environment perception, and automation. From contributing to the AdLabSens project—where I integrated radar sensors into automotive systems—to building autonomous vehicle solutions for Formula Student-Dynamics EV using ROS2, LiDAR, GPS, IMU, and SLAM technologies, I’ve explored both the research and practical sides of robotics. My experience also includes an IoT- and fiber optics–focused internship with Indian Railways, various personal robotics projects like a gyro-sensor robotic car and home automation, and enterprise experience as an SAP Security Consultant at Capgemini, where I worked on security protocols and enterprise automation.

I love working on robotics systems, graphical simulations, and hardware-software integration. My interests lie particularly in environment perception sensors, control systems, and algorithms. With a knack for technical exploration and a natural drive to problem-solve, I enjoy building robotic prototypes, supporting research experiments, and continuously learning new technologies and tools in the automation and robotics space.

When I am not working, I enjoy diving into the latest robotics research online, traveling to new places, playing PC games, and performing Bhangra — a traditional folk dance from Punjab. These activities keep me energized and curious, balancing my technical passions with fun and creativity.

Projects and Experience

Formula Student EV (Autonomous)

Developed ROS2-based software for autonomous electric racecar (FSG 2024), integrating LiDAR, GPS, IMU, and S32 processors. Enabled real-time sensor fusion, simulation in RViz, and CAN bus interfacing for reliable autonomous control.

AdLabSens Radar Integration

Installed and optimized radar sensors for all-weather vehicle perception. Developed ROS1 nodes for real-time radar data processing and fusion to enhance robotic sensing in challenging conditions.

ANSYS Simulator Experience

Engaged in live simulations and demos at ANSYS, exploring real-time autonomous vehicle technologies and Automonous vehicle simulations. Gained insights into state-of-the-art visualization tools, control systems, and high-fidelity virtual testing environments.

SAP Security Consultant – Capgemini

Worked as an Analyst in SAP Security, developing automation scripts in Python and C++ for user access management. Optimized role configurations and ensured compliance in Production, Development, and GRC environments. Enhanced monitoring through ServiceNow and automation tools, strengthening cybersecurity within automation systems.

Gyro-Sensor Robotic Car

Built a mobile-controlled robotic car using MPU6050 gyro sensors and Bluetooth module, enabling real-time directional control through Arduino-based embedded logic.

Industrial IoT Automation

Designed voice-activated automation system using NodeMCU and relays. Integrated Google Assistant and planned upgrades with ultrasonic/vision sensors for improved autonomy.
